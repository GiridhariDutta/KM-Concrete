The Problem

What's Slowing Your Team Down

We've analyzed the operational challenges common to concrete construction companies at your scale. These pain points are costing your team real time and real money.

📊

Scattered Spreadsheets

Multiple Excel files with no single version of truth — leading to outdated figures, version conflicts, and duplicated work across the estimating team.

🔗

Disconnected Tools

Estimates live in one tool, scheduling in another, and notes in email threads. Nobody has the full picture, and critical info falls through the cracks.

📱

No Mobile Access

Supervisors and PMs on-site have no real-time access to project data, forcing phone calls or driving to the office to get simple answers.

🔄

No Workflow Continuity

Moving a project forward or backward in the workflow requires manual copy-paste and re-entry, introducing errors and consuming hours each week.

📋

No Audit History

When a number changes, nobody knows who changed it or why. There's no changelog on master data, making disputes and reviews painful.

⏱️

Slow Estimating Cycle

Building a new estimate from scratch every time — no reusable assemblies, no material price library — slows your team and risks underbidding.
